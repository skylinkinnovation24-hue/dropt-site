ENGINE 01 · Pincode → Promise

Eligibility

Customer enters a location and POST /check-delivery-eta fires. A sub-300ms response surfaces a ⚡30-60 min or ⚡up-to-3-hour badge before checkout — turning delivery speed into a conversion lever.

POST /check-delivery-eta { "pincode": "560001", "cart": [{ "sku": "TSH-BLK-M" }] } → 287ms { "promise": "⚡ 42 min" }
ENGINE 02 · Geo-aware stock

Inventory

Real-time stock lookup across stores and warehouses, Redis-cached for speed. Inventory is reserved and locked the moment an order is placed — so the promise is never broken.

GET /stock?geo=12.97,77.59 stores: [ Indiranagar ✓ 4 Koramangala ✓ 2 ] reserve → LOCKED
ENGINE 03 · Smart routing

OMS / WMS

Routes orders into Shopify, Unicommerce or Browntape and auto-allocates them to the hyperlocal fulfillment queue — no manual triage, no spreadsheet handoffs.

route(order) ├─ Shopify ├─ Unicommerce └─ Browntape queue: HYPERLOCAL
ENGINE 04 · Staff app

Store Fulfillment

A React Native app gives store staff a priority queue, an SLA countdown timer and a guided pack → handoff flow. The shop floor becomes a micro-fulfillment node.

STORE APP queue ▸ 3 orders SLA ▸ 06:12 ⏱ status ▸ PACKING
ENGINE 05 · Broadcast & accept

Rider Dispatch

Proprietary broadcast-and-accept dispatch with ETA scoring, auto-reassignment on timeout and surge handling — tuned for fashion drops, not 10-minute grocery sprints.

broadcast → 5 riders accept ◂ R-204 ETA score: 0.91 reassign if > 45s
ENGINE 06 · Live to door

Tracking & Proof

WebSocket GPS tracking on a branded page hosted on the brand’s own domain, closed out with OTP, photo proof and geo-fence verification at the doorstep.

ws://track/ORD-8841 lat/lng ▸ live OTP ▸ 4471 ✓ photo ▸ captured ✓
⚡Dropt Promise: 30-60 min or up-to-3-hour delivery — selected before the customer ever clicks Pay.
