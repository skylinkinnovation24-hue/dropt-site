Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data Dropt collects, how we use and protect it, and the rights you have over your information when you use our platform and services.

Last updated: 2025

1. Introduction

Skylink Innovation ("Dropt", "we", "us" or "our") operates a hyperlocal delivery platform that enables brands to fulfil same-day orders from their existing retail stores. We are committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who interacts with our platform — including brand operators, store staff, delivery partners and end customers. This Privacy Policy describes our practices for handling personal data and applies to our website, dashboards, mobile applications and APIs.

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy. If you do not agree with it, please discontinue use of our platform. This policy should be read together with our Terms of Service and our DPDP Compliance statement.

2. Information We Collect

Account information. When you or your organisation register for Dropt, we collect details such as your name, business name, email address, phone number, role, and authentication credentials.

Usage information. We collect data about how you interact with our dashboards and apps, including pages viewed, features used, actions taken, timestamps and session activity.

Device and technical information. We may collect IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and, for delivery partners, approximate or precise location data required to dispatch and track orders.

Order and delivery information. To fulfil deliveries we process order details, delivery addresses, recipient contact numbers, one-time verification codes, and proof-of-delivery records such as photographs and timestamps.

3. How We Use Information

We use personal data to provide, operate and improve our services. This includes routing orders to the nearest store, assigning delivery partners, calculating delivery times, enabling live tracking, verifying deliveries, providing customer and operational support, processing payments, detecting and preventing fraud, maintaining security, complying with legal obligations, and communicating service-related updates. We process data only for purposes that are necessary and proportionate to delivering the platform.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

Service providers. We share data with trusted third parties who support our operations — such as cloud hosting, payment gateways, communication providers and mapping services — strictly to the extent required to perform their functions, and under contractual confidentiality and data-protection obligations.

Brand customers. Where Dropt processes data on behalf of a brand, the relevant order and delivery information is made available to that brand to operate its own deliveries.

Legal and safety. We may disclose information where required by law, regulation, legal process or governmental request, or where necessary to protect the rights, safety and property of Dropt, our users or the public. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. These include encryption in transit, access controls, tenant isolation between brands, role-based permissions, audit logging and regular security reviews. While no system can be guaranteed to be fully secure, we work continuously to safeguard the data entrusted to us.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as it is necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, to comply with our legal, accounting and regulatory obligations, to resolve disputes and to enforce our agreements. When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymise it. Retention periods vary depending on the category of data and the applicable legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (the "DPDP Act"), you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data, and to request erasure of your data where it is no longer required. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, and nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity. To exercise these rights, or to raise a grievance, please contact us using the details below; we will respond within the timelines prescribed by law.

8. Cookies

Our website and dashboards use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, maintain session security and understand how our services are used. You can control cookies through your browser settings, although disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the platform.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can take appropriate steps to delete it in accordance with applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@dropt.in. Skylink Innovation is based in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.
