About Dropt

Boilerplate.

Dropt is white-label hyperlocal delivery infrastructure for D2C, retail and lifestyle brands. It turns a brand's existing stores into a same-day delivery network — no dark stores, no marketplace tax — connecting their website, Shopify and WMS/OMS so orders route to the nearest store, get packed, and are delivered by riders in minutes. Dropt is based in Bangalore, India, and was founded in 2025.
